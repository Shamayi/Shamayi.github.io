Analysis of Ecological Aesthetics Thought

  • In the film, Na'i, as the intelligent creature of Pandora, they reverence nature and conform to the laws of nature, which is a perfect interpretation of the aesthetic proposition of "poetic habitation" proposed by Heidegger.
  • Nami is in awe of nature, regards herself and other creatures as equal creatures in nature, and follows the natural will and balance of Eva ——, the god of nature. Their attitude towards nature and the equal mode of coexistence with nature are the presentation of the "complex charm" picture advocated by ecological aesthetics.

    Rebellion against anthropocentrism

  • The collapse of the human beings in the film is the recognition of respecting all things and knowing how to live in harmony with nature, the generous praise of the beauty, and also the deconstruction of human-centrism and the worship of ecological-centrism.
  • In the film, human beings paid a painful price for their greed and contempt for ecology, and finally were forced to return to the desolate earth, while the vigilant human representatives were fully integrated into the alien civilization with respect for nature. On the one hand, this shows the film creators' resistance to anthropocentrism, and also shows their good expectations for human beings to go back to nature
